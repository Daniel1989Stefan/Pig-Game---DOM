Rules:

  • You can roll the dice as many times as you want.
  • If you roll a 1, you lose all the points in your current "Score", and the turn passes to the other player.
  • If you have points in your Score, you can press "Add to Total Score" to save them and add to your "Total Score".
  • The first player to reach 100 points in the "Total Score" wins.
